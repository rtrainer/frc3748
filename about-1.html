ABOUT THE TEAM

Building Robots. Building Leaders.

WHO WE ARE

We are Team 3748 Ragnarok Robotics, a student-led robotics team from Mt. Hebron High School based in Ellicott City, MD.

Founded with a passion for STEM, our team competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Every year, we are challenged to build an industrial-size robot to play a difficult field game in alliance with other teams, while also fundraising to meet our goals, designing a team brand, and advancing respect and appreciation for STEM within the local community.

OUR MISSION

We foster encouraging environments for students to apply engineering concepts to real-world challenges. We believe in:

  • Hands-on Learning: From machining to coding.
  • Collaboration: Working together to solve complex problems.
  • Innovation: Pushing the boundaries of what high school students can achieve.
